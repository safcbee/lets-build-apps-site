# Portfolio Alignment Report

## Portfolio Positioning

Let's Build Apps is now presented as a family of simple apps with focused jobs. The homepage hero introduces learning, planning, routines, travel, countdowns, and coffee as one coherent portfolio rather than a loose app list.

## Product Ownership

| App | Owns | Positioning used on site |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Let's Build Sentences | Learning, language development, sentence building, child-friendly education. | Helping children learn through calm sentence building and handwriting practice. |
| Let's Build Countdowns | Excitement, anticipation, upcoming events, important moments. | Making future events visible and exciting. |
| Let's Build Travel Plans | Travel organisation, trip preparation, itineraries, packing, travel information. | Countdowns helps you look forward to the trip. Travel Plans helps you get ready for it. |
| Let's Build Good Habits | Daily routines, habit building, consistency, personal improvement. | Helping build better routines one day at a time. |
| Let's Build Perfect Coffee | Coffee recipes, brew tracking, routines, experimentation, improvement. | Helping coffee lovers make every cup a little better. |

## Overlap Removed

- Countdowns is framed around anticipation and future moments.
- Travel Plans is framed around preparation, saved places, packing, and itineraries.
- Good Habits no longer claims social or community features.
- Perfect Coffee no longer appears as a generic lifestyle utility or Portaflow-branded product.
- Sentences stays focused on language and learning rather than broad educational platform claims.

## Portaflow Migration

Portaflow is not shown as a primary product. A legacy `/portaflow/` page now redirects visitors to `/perfect-coffee/` and explains that Portaflow has evolved into Let's Build Perfect Coffee.

## Placeholder Removal

The homepage no longer publishes future placeholder cards for unrelated concept products. App pages no longer expose unsupported Android downloads, free-app promises, fake demos, live editor counts, or social proof.
