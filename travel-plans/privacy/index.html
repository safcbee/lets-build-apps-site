Privacy Policy

Let's Build Travel Plans

Last updated 29 June 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Let's Build Travel Plans (the “App”) collects, uses, stores and protects your information. The App is developed and operated by SAFCMedia (Brian Leary) (“we”, “us”, “our”). By using the App you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information we collect

We collect only the information needed to run the App's family trip-planning features:

DataWhy we collect it
Name (provided through Sign in with Apple) To create your profile and show who you are to other members of your family space.
Trip data — trips, itineraries, costs, to-do items, wallet documents and chat messages This is the content you create in the App. It is shared with the members of your family space so you can plan together.
Photos you add to trip memories Stored so they appear in your trip and are visible to your family space.
Device token for push notifications To deliver push notifications (for example, new messages or trip updates) to your device.

We do not collect your email address from Sign in with Apple in a usable form if you choose Apple's private relay, and we do not sell your data, use it for advertising, or track you across other apps or websites.

2. Sign in with Apple

Authentication is handled by Apple through Sign in with Apple. Apple verifies your identity and provides us with a secure identifier and, with your permission, your name. We never see or store your Apple password. You can manage how Sign in with Apple is used in your device's Apple ID settings.

3. Where your data is stored

Your data is stored using Supabase, our backend and database provider, on servers located in the European Union (EU region). Photos and wallet documents are stored in private storage that is only accessible to authenticated members of your family space. Access is enforced by row-level security so that data is only visible to the family space it belongs to.

4. Subscriptions and payments

Any subscriptions or in-app purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not collect, see, or store your payment card details or billing information. Apple's handling of payment data is governed by Apple's own privacy policy.

5. How we use your information

  • To provide and operate the App's trip-planning and family-sharing features.
  • To sync your trips, costs, itineraries, photos and messages across the members of your family space.
  • To send push notifications you have agreed to receive.
  • To maintain the security and integrity of the service.

6. Sharing within a family space

The App is built around shared family spaces. Content you add to a trip (itineraries, costs, to-do items, chat messages, photos and documents) is visible to the other members of that family space. Only people you invite with your family invite code can join your space.

7. Data retention and account deletion

You can delete your account at any time from within the App: open the You tab and choose Delete account. Deleting your account removes your profile and your push notification device tokens, and signs you out.

Please note that trips and other content belonging to your family space are shared data. Deleting your account removes you from the family space but does not delete trips or other family data, so that the remaining members are not affected. If you wish to have shared content removed entirely, please contact us.

8. Security

Data is transmitted over encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) and stored in access-controlled infrastructure. Push notifications are delivered through Apple Push Notification service (APNs). While no system can be guaranteed completely secure, we take reasonable measures to protect your information.

9. Children's privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age required to hold an Apple ID in their region. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without appropriate consent.

10. Your rights

Depending on your location (including under the EU General Data Protection Regulation), you may have the right to access, correct, export or delete your personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise these rights, use the in-app account deletion feature or contact us using the details below.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

12. Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us:

  • Email: support@leary.cloud
  • Website: apps.leary.cloud
