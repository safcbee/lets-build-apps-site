MASTER GOAL — LET’S BUILD APPS WEBSITE V2

Mission

Transform apps.leary.cloud from a simple app showcase into the canonical public home of the Let’s Build Apps portfolio.

The final result should feel like a polished, coherent product family with a consistent identity, clear product ownership, accurate feature descriptions, strong navigation, and zero placeholder content.

This is not a cosmetic refresh.

This is a portfolio-wide alignment exercise.

⸻

Strategic Objective

Every visitor should immediately understand:

* What Let’s Build Apps is
* What each app does
* Why each app exists
* How the apps differ
* How the apps work together

The website should communicate:

Simple apps with focused jobs.

No app should feel duplicated.

No app should compete with another app.

No page should contain placeholder content, dead functionality, or generic template artefacts.

⸻
